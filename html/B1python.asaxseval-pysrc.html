 84      """Calculate the pure resonant as the "difference of differences" 
 85       
 86      Inputs: 
 87          I1,I2,I3: intensity curves for the three energies 
 88          DI1,DI2,DI3: error data for the intensity curves 
 89          f11,f12,f13: f' values 
 90          f21,f22,f23: f'' values 
 91       
 92      Outputs: 
 93          sep12: (I1-I2)/(f11-f12) 
 94          dsep12: error of sep12 
 95          sep23: (I2-I3)/(f12-f13) 
 96          dsep23: error of I2-I3 
 97          R: the pure resonant term 
 98          DR: the error of the pure resonant term 
 99      """ 
100      factor=f11-f13+(f22**2-f21**2)/(f12-f11)-(f22**2-f23**2)/(f12-f13) 
101      DR=np.sqrt((DI1*DI1)/(f12-f11)**2+ 
102                    (DI2*DI2)*(1/(f12-f11)**2+1/(f12-f13)**2)+ 
103                    (DI3*DI3)/(f12-f13)**2)/np.absolute(factor); 
104      sep12=(I1-I2)/(f11-f12) 
105      sep23=(I2-I3)/(f12-f13) 
106      R=(sep12 -sep23)/factor; 
107      dsep12=np.absolute(np.sqrt((DI1*DI1)+(DI2*DI2))/(f11-f12)) 
108      dsep23=np.absolute(np.sqrt((DI2*DI2)+(DI3*DI3))/(f12-f13)) 
109      return sep12,dsep12,sep23,dsep23,R,DR
